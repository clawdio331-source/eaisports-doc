# Why EAISports?

### Turn your AI subscription into a revenue stream.

---

## The Opportunity

Millions of people pay for ChatGPT Plus or Pro every month. Most use it for chat. EAISports gives that subscription a new purpose: building games that earn money while you sleep.

---

## What EAISports Changes

### 1. Your ChatGPT Subscription Becomes Productive
Stop paying $20/month for a chatbot. Start using it to build games that attract players and generate `$EAISPORTS` tokens. Every game you publish is a new revenue stream.

### 2. AI Does the Heavy Lifting
You don't need to know React, JavaScript, or game development. Describe your idea to the AI agent and iterate through conversation. The agent handles scaffolding, coding, and debugging.

### 3. Zero-Friction Deployment
No cloud accounts, no CI/CD pipelines, no hosting configuration. One command — `eaisports push` — and your game is live with a shareable URL, auto-generated icon, and a spot in the arcade.

### 4. Built-In Distribution
Every game published to EAISports is instantly discoverable in the arcade browser and on the desktop. Players don't need to find your website or download anything.

### 5. Real Economics
The points and token system rewards creators for building games that people play. More visitors means more tokens. This creates a flywheel: better games attract more players, which rewards creators, which attracts more creators.

### 6. Not Locked to One AI
ChatGPT is the easiest starting point, but EAISports supports 7 providers. Use OpenRouter for access to Claude, Gemini, and 100+ models. Use Nous Research for open-source models. Or bring your own OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

### 7. Reusable Skills
The skills system means you're never starting from scratch. Use community-built templates for common game types (trivia, puzzles, interactive fiction, arcade, mobile) and focus on what makes your game unique.
