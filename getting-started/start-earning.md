# Start Earning

### How the money flows in EAISports.

---

## The Earning Model

EAISports rewards both sides of the ecosystem. Whether you build games or play them, there's a path to earning `$EAISPORTS` tokens.

---

## For Creators

### Visitor Rewards
Earn **10 points per unique daily visitor** across all your published games. More popular games = more daily income.

### Milestone Bonuses
Hit visitor thresholds and unlock one-time token rewards:

| Unique Visitors | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 100 | 500 `$EAISPORTS` |
| 500 | 3,000 `$EAISPORTS` |
| 1,000 | 8,000 `$EAISPORTS` |
| 5,000 | 50,000 `$EAISPORTS` |

Each game has its own independent milestone track, so publishing multiple successful games compounds your rewards.

### The Math
A single game hitting 5,000 visitors earns you **61,500 `$EAISPORTS`** in milestone bonuses alone, plus daily visitor points on top. Publish 5 viral games and you're looking at **300,000+ tokens**.

---

## For Players

### Daily Play Rewards
Earn **10 points per game per day** just for playing. Play 5 different games a day, earn 50 points.

### Leaderboard Prizes
Top 3 players on each game's daily leaderboard share a prize pool:

```
Pool = max(500, daily_unique_visitors × 10)
```

| Rank | Share |
| --- | --- |
| 1st | 50% |
| 2nd | 30% |
| 3rd | 20% |

Higher traffic games have bigger prize pools. A game with 100 daily visitors has a **1,000-point pool**.

### Referral Bonuses
Invite friends and earn from their activity:

| Who | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| **You (invitee)** | Permanent **20% boost** on all your play points |
| **Your referrer (L1)** | **20%** of your play points as bonus |
| **Their referrer (L2)** | **10%** of your play points as bonus |

---

## Getting Started

1. **Connect your Solana wallet** at [eaisports.ai](https://eaisports.ai) — this enables point tracking
2. **Build a game** (`eaisports push`) or **play games** on the platform
3. **Share your referral code** to grow your network and earn passive bonuses
4. **Track everything** on the [dashboard](https://eaisports.ai/dashboard) or via `eaisports stats`
