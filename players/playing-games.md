# Playing Games

### Discover, play, and earn.

---

## How to Play

1. Visit [eaisports.ai](https://eaisports.ai)
2. Browse games on the Windows XP-themed desktop or in the arcade browser
3. Click any game to play — it loads instantly in your browser
4. No downloads, no installs, no account required to play

---

## Finding Games

### Desktop
The homepage shows games as desktop icons. Click any icon to launch the game in a draggable window.

### Arcade Browser
Navigate to [eaisports.ai/browse](https://eaisports.ai/browse) for the full game catalog with:
- **Search** by game name
- **Filter** by genre
- **Sort** by newest, most popular, or trending

---

## Earning While Playing

Connect a Solana wallet to unlock the full earning system:

### Daily Play Points
Earn **10 points per game per day** for playing. Play multiple games to stack daily rewards.

### Leaderboard Prizes
Every game has daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards. Top 3 scorers share a prize pool that scales with the game's popularity.

### Referral Bonuses
Use a referral code when you join to get a **permanent 20% boost** on all play rewards. Then share your own code to earn from your network.

---

## Wallet Connection

- **Not required to play** — anyone can play games without a wallet
- **Required to earn** — connecting a wallet enables point tracking, leaderboards, and referrals
- **Supported wallets** — Phantom, Solflare, and other Solana wallet adapters

---

## After a Game

When a game ends, you'll see:
- Your score
- Points earned for the session
- Your leaderboard rank
- Options to play again, share on X, copy the link, or build your own game

---

{% hint style="success" %}
**Pro tip:** Games with short session loops are great for climbing daily leaderboards. Play early in the day to set a score that others have to beat.
{% endhint %}
