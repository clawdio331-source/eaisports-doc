# Vision

### From browser games to a full game development studio.

---

## The Thesis

> **Game creation is becoming a conversation, not a craft.** As AI models improve, the gap between "having an idea" and "shipping a playable game" will shrink to minutes.

EAISports is built for this future — a platform where the creative vision matters more than technical skill, and where distribution and monetization are built in from the start.

---

## Where We're Going

### Phase 1: Browser Games (Now)
Today, EAISports turns your ChatGPT subscription into a browser game studio. Build, deploy, and earn — all from the terminal. The web platform at eaisports.ai provides instant distribution and a built-in player base.

### Phase 2: Cross-Platform Games (Next)
The same CLI that builds browser games will package them for traditional distribution channels. Build a game with AI, then export it for:
- **Steam** — Desktop games for PC and Mac
- **App Store** — iOS games
- **Google Play** — Android games
- **Itch.io** — Indie game distribution

The AI agent handles the platform-specific packaging, store requirements, and submission process.

### Phase 3: Full Development Studio (Future)
EAISports becomes a complete game development studio CLI. Anyone with an AI subscription can:
- Build games through conversation
- Test across platforms
- Package for any distribution channel
- Publish to stores worldwide
- Earn revenue from sales and engagement

No game development experience needed. No team required. Just you, your AI, and your ideas.

---

## What We're Building Toward

### AI-Native Creation
Today, the AI agent handles scaffolding and coding while the creator directs. Tomorrow, the agent will handle entire game design cycles — from concept to deployment — with minimal human input. The skills system is the foundation: reusable patterns that teach the agent what good games look like.

### Community-Driven Ecosystem
Skills create a flywheel where the best game patterns are extracted, shared, and remixed. Top creators publish skills that help newcomers build better games faster. The skills registry evolves into a marketplace where proven templates have real value.

### Programmable Economics
Points and `$EAISPORTS` tokens are the first step. The long-term vision is a full token economy where:
- Creators earn from game popularity and store sales
- Players earn from engagement
- Skill authors earn from template usage
- The platform captures value from the ecosystem's growth

### Autonomous Leagues
As AI models become capable of end-to-end game creation, EAISports can host autonomous generation events — AI systems competing to build the most engaging games, evaluated by real player engagement metrics rather than static benchmarks.

---

## Why This Matters

The intersection of AI capability and game creation is unique:

1. **Games are the ultimate test of AI creativity** — they require visual design, logic, user experience, and entertainment value
2. **Games have built-in engagement metrics** — player retention and visitor counts are objective quality signals
3. **Games are inherently shareable** — viral distribution is natural, not forced
4. **The feedback loop is fast** — build, deploy, measure, iterate in the same session
5. **Distribution channels exist** — Steam, App Store, and Google Play already have billions of users waiting for games

EAISports captures all five: AI builds the game, the platform distributes it, real players evaluate it, and the creator earns — starting with browser games today and scaling to every platform tomorrow.
